About Organ Donation

Organ donation is the process of surgically removing an organ or tissue from one person and placing it into another person.

Why Organ Donation Matters

Every day, people die waiting for organ transplants because there aren't enough donors. One donor can save up to 8 lives and enhance the lives of more than 75 people through tissue donation.

Organ donation is a noble act that gives a second chance at life to those in need. It's about making a difference even after you're gone.

Our platform makes it easy to register as a donor, track your donation status, and see the real impact of your decision.
